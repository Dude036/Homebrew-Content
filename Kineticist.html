Elemental Attunement

Your inherit nature ties you to an Element, and stems from your tie to an Elemental Plane. You gain a feat from your Elemental Attunement subclass at 1st, 4th, 7th, 11th, and 15th.

  • Earth
  • Water
  • Fire
  • Air
  • Psychic

Kinetic Attacks

You gain the following Actions.

Kinetic Blast
Burn: 0
You tap into your well of elemental energy, and direct it into a singular point. You fire a blast of energy at a target within 30 ft, make a ranged attack against the targets AC. On a hit, deal an amount of damage equal to your Kinetic Blast Damage.
Kinetic Burst
Burn: +1
Gathering energy on a single point within 30 ft, you create an explosion of energy around that point to all creatures within your Burst Radius. All targets must makes a Dexterity saving throw against your Burn DC. On a fail, deal an amount of damage equal to your Kinetic Burst Damage. On a success, the target takes half damage, and no additional effects are applied.
Focus Energy (Bonus)
Burn: -1

If both of your hands are free, you may spend your Bonus action to reduce the the amount of Burn taken by 1. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

If you take damage before you have released the Focused Energy via a Kinetic Attack, you must make a Constitution save to maintain your gathered power. The DC for the save is equal to 10 or Half of the damage taken, whichever is larger.

Focus Energy (Bonus + Action)
Burn: -2

If both of your hands are free, you may spend your Action and Bonus Action to reduce the the amount of Burn taken by 2. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

If you take damage before you have released the Focused Energy via a Kinetic Attack, you must make a Constitution save to maintain your gathered power. The DC for the save is equal to 10 or Half of the damage taken, whichever is larger.

Focus Energy (Full Round)
Burn: -3

If both of your hands are free, you may spend the entirety of your turn to reduce the the amount of Burn taken by 3. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

If you take damage before you have released the Focused Energy via a Kinetic Attack, you must make a Constitution save to maintain your gathered power. The DC for the save is equal to 10 or Half of the damage taken, whichever is larger.

Note: Focus Energy is extremely loud, and volatile. You shed Bright light up to 20 ft in radius, and Dim light an additional 20 ft past that.

Burn

Your Spellcasting comes at a cost of your own Constitution. Whenever you take an action that incurs burn, you decrease your total HP by double the amount of Burn accepted. i.e. 2 Burn is used, reduce maximum HP by 4. You cannot accept Burn Damage if that damage would reduce your HP to less than 1 HP. Your Max HP is reset when you take a long rest.

When casting a Kinetic Attack that requires a DC, your DC is calculated as 8 + Proficiency Bonus + Constitution Modifer + Burn Points Used.

Elemental Defense

Beginning at 2nd level, If your Maximum HP is reduced as a result of inflicting Burn upon yourself. You are bolstered by your Elements, and recieve an AC bonus equal to your Constitution Modifier if you are unarmored.

Kinetic Advantage

Beginning at 3rd level, at the start of your turn, you may accept 1 point of Burn damage to add your Constitution Modifier to all Kinetic Attacks made this round. This Burn damage cannot be reduced by way of Focus Energy.

Elemental Attunement Feat

At 4th level, you gain an additional feat from your Elemental Attunement.

Ability Score Improvement

When you reach 4th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

If your DM allows the use of feats, you may instead take a feat.

Meta-Kinesis (Empower)

At 5th Level, you may accept 1 point of Burn damage to increase the damage dealt by a Kinetic Attack by 1 for every damage dice rolled.

Kinetic Familiarity I

When you reach 6th level, Whenever making an action that uses Burn, you reduce the total amount of burn taken by 1, to a minimum of 0.

At 14th Level, your reservoir of Focus Energy is deeper. You may spend 1 minute to prepare Focus Energy as if it were a Full Round action. This reservoir persists until used, or you take a long rest. You may use this reservoir at any time during the day as a free action.

Warping the elements around your has become second nature. Once per long rest, you may choose to ignore all burn on a single Kinetic Attack.

True Kineticist

At 20th level, you've grown accustomed to burn and its effect. If you have taken burn damage since your last rest and enter combat, you recover 1d8 HP.

Ability Score Improvement

When you reach 20th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

If your DM allows the use of feats, you may instead take a feat.

Level: 7  Burn: 0

As an action, you can create an icy trail that follows your movement, and leaves a trial of ice behind. You can choose to ascend upward at 45 degress at half speed, or decsend at 45 degrees at twice your speed. Your movement is not restricted to moving along the ground.

This effect lasts for a number of rounds equal to your Kineticist level. The ice trail you leave behind melts after 1 round.

The target also takes damage as if it fell the distance it travelled.

Burn: Varies

You may accept any number of points of Burn to raise Pillars of Ice that occupy 10 x 10 squares, with a height of 40 ft. These Pillars have an AC equal to half your Kineticist Level rounded up, and have a number of HP equal to 2 x your Kineticist Level. These Pillars can be placed under creatures, who may make a Dexterity Saving throw to dodge out of the way. Creatures who succeed the Dexterity saving throw must move off of the Pillar's square as part of the save.

Any creature who starts their turn on top of a column takes 4d8 cold damage if they are not wearing shoes, and 2d8 if they are.

Acid Rain
Prerequisite: Earth or Air Attunement
Burn: 1+

You point at a location within 90 ft of you, and create a rain cloud. The rain cloud rains acid on all creatures in a 10 foot radius. You may accept up to 3 additional points of burn to increase the radius by 10 ft per poitn of Burn accepted. The Acid cloud persists for a number of rounds equal to your Constitution modifer, and can be moved 30ft as a Bonus action.

All creatures caught in the Rain must make a Constitution Saving throw. On a sucessful save, the target takes half of your Kinetic Burst Damage.

Terra's Spine
Prerequisite: Fire or Earth Attunement
Burn: 2

A 60 ft line of Obsidian shards jut out of the ground, ripping into all creature's feet. Deal damage equal to your Kinetic Burst, with an additional 3d6 Peircing Damage.

Rubble
Prerequisite: Earth or Water Attunement
Burn: 1

A large tremor shakes the earth around you in a 50 ft radius. All creatures within the effect take 1d6 slashing damage. Whenever a creature moves 5 ft, the sharp earth beneath their feet pierces them. Every 5 ft of movement incurs an additional 1d6 slashing damage. This effect lasts for a number of rounds equal to your Constitution Modifier.
