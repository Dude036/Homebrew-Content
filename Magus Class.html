Magus Class

The Magus is not someone who would study from old tomes in a musty library, or sit in a classroom full of pompous wizards. The Magus trains not only their mind, but their body as well. The secrets of magic for them, are told through plunging their steel into the flesh of their enemies.

Additional Feats

Sometimes you want additional Feats for your magus to adept them to new scenarios. These feats allow you to do just that.

Monastic Magus
  • You can now use your Spellstrike ability with an unarmed strike.
  • Your Unarmed strike now deals 1d4 + Your strength modifier, instead of your Strength modifier.
  • At level 8, your Unarmed Strike deals 1d6 instead of 1d4. At level 15, your Unarmed Strike now deals 1d8.
