College of Cuisine

Chef's Pack
At 3rd level, you can during a Long or a Short rest, prepare a meal for your party which grants them extraordinary benefits. You gain proficiency with Chef's Utensils. You may cook up to 2 types of meals, and may cook a number of meals equal to 4 + your proficiency modifier. Below is a table of potential meals you can create.
MealDescription
Spicy Meatballs You char your friend's tongue, and grant them resistance to Fire damage until the end of their next long rest.
Sweet Cream Your dessert chills your skin, and grants them resistance to Cold Damage until the end of their next long rest.
Comfort Food Your food has healing properties, which heals for a number of HP equal to your proficiency bonus plus your Charisma Modifier. This can only be applied once per Short Rest.
Brain Food Your food enhances the properties of your brain, which grants an additional bardic inspiration, which can be used until the end of their next long rest.
Quick Snack
At 3rd Level, you've always got food on hand. So long as you've prepared enough food, you can give a meal to someone as a bonus action. Then can consume the snack as a reaction and gain an additional 1d4 to any roll during their following turn. You can make as many snacks as you have time to prep. A snack takes 1 minute to make, and costs 1 gp. The snack loses its effects after 24 hours, or until you take a long rest.
