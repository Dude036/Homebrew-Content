Airship Battles

Airship Battles are the pinnacle of Steampunk adventures. Typically these take place on a cartesian grid, and high up in the sky. These are sometimes known as Dogfights.

Airship Construction

An Airship is built with 6 key points in mind. Size, Armor, Shields, Lift, Motor and Armaments.

Size

This category decides the size of your Aircraft. Each square of the playing grid is 100' by 100', so keep that in mind when making your Airship. Size determine's Base Maneuverability (Move PEN), Base Occupancy, Base Equipment Capacity, and Base Hull Points (HP).

Dogfighting Rules

Dogfighting is when various ships engage in air to air fighting. Here are the General Rules for Dogfighting;

General Rules
  • Each airship has a number of action points to use per round of combat, which do not roll over between rounds. The number of action points is also based on the crew size.
  • There are 4 Phases to a fight, all of which happen at the same time, Each round of combat lasts about a minute, where each phase may be occupied the entire duration, or for a few split seconds.
  • Crew members are limited to actions within their Phase, but can rotate out to a new station in between Rounds. A Gunner can swap to the Crew at the end of the round, but someone on the Deck phase cannot pilot during the same round.
  • Any Damage dealt during round is applied at the end of the round in one lump sum. If your Pilot takes a Flyby Attack during the pilot phase, that damage is applied after the gunner phase.
Crew Phase

The Goal of the Crew Phase is Preperation, such as shield regeneration/redistributing, hull patching, reload heavy weapons, etc...

Shield Regeneration
Actions:
Details: the crew charges the shield with magical energy. Regenerate an amount equal to your shield's Recharge Capacity.
Hull Repair
Actions:
Details: Repair one broken condition on the ship.
Deck Phase

The Goal of the Deck Phase is Buff and Debuff your allies and enemies. Calling an enemy a particularly offensive name, aggrivating an enemy to attack only you, etc...

Pilot Phase

The Goal of the Pilot Phase is Positioning for the Gunner Phase. Align your weapons towards your enemies, allowing them to be fired.

Gunner Phase

The Goal of the Gunner Phase is Damage. Remember all damage is applied at the end of the round, not when dice are rolled.
