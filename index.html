Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition Content

Gunslinger Class

A homebrew gunslinger class focusing on a gunslinger's knowledge of their firearm. Sublcasses include;

  • Close Quarters Combatant: An Agile pistol whipping menace to enemies.
  • Spellslinger: Cantrip wielding mage who attunes their bullets and abilites to use magic instead of regular steel
  • Demolitionist: An explosives expert with a variety of tools at their disposal
  • Sniper: A long range damage dealer that specialized in inhibiting an enemy's abilites while tearing through them.
Item Enchantment Rules

Have you ever wanted to create specialty items? Or enchant a weapon with additional properties? Or maybe add a spell to a weapon or set of armor? Look no further than this here table. It contains a list of rules on how that can be achieved.

Masterwork Traits

Masterworked weapons increases the damage potential of Martial characters to attempt to keep up with Magic wielders. These are traits ported form Pathfinder 1e into D&d 5e. This also contains masterworked armor, which enhances armor in similair methods to that of Pathfinder 1e.

Demigod Prestige Class

D&D 5e lacks prestiges classes, which is a shame. This is an additional level on top of your player level to help you create a Demigod. Become an unintended consequence of you favorite diety meddling with mortals.

Lycan Prestige Class

D&D 5e lacks prestiges classes, which is a shame. This is an additional level on top of your player level to help you create a Lycan, otherwise called a Werewolf. Whether born from a curse, or bitten, you can become your favorite furry friend.

Vampires Template

A Template is a set of abilities/flaws born from something that your character is. Vampires don't evolve or change based on how long they've been a vampire, but on how many times they've fed and grown their coven.

Bard Subclass: College of Cuisine

Become a fry pan weilding Bard known for their cooking prowess. Heal your allies beside a campfire, throw too spicy food into the mouth of an unwitting enemy, or bolster your friends against the ails of a chilly night of winter. Do all this and more!
