Prestige Classes

Prestige classes are classes you get upon meeting a certain prerequisite. You can only take one Prestige class per character, and cannot change it except by extraordinary circumstances. You gain one level in your Prestige class for every two levels in your normal classes. For example, a 5th level fighter, has 2 levels in their Prestige class. A multiclassed 5th level fighter & 3rd level sorcerer, has 4 levels in their Prestige class. Below are potential Prestige classes.

Diety Domain I

Each diety has a domain. You may select one of the following domain abilities from the list of Domains your diety is over or associated with. You may also select a domain from your diety's alignment.

Air Domain
Level 1
If you are able to cast spells, you now know Feather Fall. This does not count towards known spells. You are also able to cast it once per day without expending a spell slot. You cannot use the ability again until you finish a long rest.
Level 2
You gain a Fly speed equal to your movement speed. While flying, you cannot dash, or extend your movement speed faster than your regular movement speed. You cannot weild two handed weapons, wear heavy or medium armor, or carry more than 5 lbs in your hands while flying. You also cannot fly if you are encumbered.
Level 3
Your Fly speed is now equal to two times your movement speed. You can move and act normally now, unless you are encumbered, in which case you cannot get airborn.
Level 4
You are unaffected by gusts, winds, fogs, and tornadoes. Spells such as Gust, or Cloudkill do not affect you. You also can hold your breathe indefinitely without penalty.
Level 5
You create a tornado originating around yourself. All creatures within 20 ft of you must make a Strength Saving throw or be thrown away from you. The DC of the save is determined by the creatures distance from you. From 5 - 10 ft, the DC is 10 + Wisdom Modifier + Proficiency bonus. From 15 - 20 ft, the DC is the DC is 10 + Wisdom Modifier + Proficiency bonus. If the creature saves, they may act normally. If the creature fails, they may not move closer to you, but act normally otherwise. If the creature fails by 5 or more, they are pushed to the extent of your tornado, and cannot move back into the tornado.
Animal Domain
Level 1
You now have the ability to speak with Animals. So long as a creature has an Intelligence score greater than 1, you can speak with them and they understand your intent. If the creature has an intelligence score of 4 or greater, then can speak back to you.
Level 2
At the end of a long rest, you gain the ability to transform some part of your body and gain one of the following natural attacks.
  • Bite: You gain a natural bite attack dealing 1d8 + Strength Piercing damage. At level 6 the damage increases to 2d8 + STR. At level 12, 3d8 + STR. At level 20, 4d8 + STR.
  • Talon: You gain a natural claw attack dealing 1d8 + Strength Slashing damage. At level 6 the damage increases to 2d8 + STR. At level 12, 3d8 + STR. At level 20, 4d8 + STR.
  • Headbutt: You gain a natural charge attack dealing 1d8 + Strength Bludgeoning damage. At level 6 the damage increases to 2d8 + STR. At level 12, 3d8 + STR. At level 20, 4d8 + STR.
Level 3
Once per long rest, you gain the ability to Polymorph into a creature of the Beast subtype whose CR is less than or equal to 1 CR.
Level 4
You gain the ability to Polymorph into a creature of the Beast subtype whose CR is less than or equal to 3.
Level 5
You gain the ability to Polymorph into a creature of the Beast subtype whose CR is less than or equal to 6.
Artifice Domain
Level 1
You gain the ability to cast Mending. You use your Intelligence for this casting.
Level 2
As an action, you can create a Tiny non-magical object with the following effects: *Note, this effect is permenant until you create another effect or dismiss it as an action.
  • The object sheds bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet.
  • Whenever tapped by a creature, the object emits a recorded message that can be heard up to 10 feet away. You utter the message when you bestow this property on the object, and the recording can be no more than 6 seconds long.
  • The object continuously emits your choice of an odor or a nonverbal sound (wind, waves, chirping, or the like). The chosen phenomenon is perceivable up to 10 feet away.
  • A static visual effect appears on one of the object's surfaces. This effect can be a picture, up to 25 words of text, lines and shapes, or a mixture of these elements, as you like.
Level 3
You are able to create basic automatons. Once per long rest, you can cast Find Familiar as a ritual, but instead of finding a natural animal form, you create an automaton in the shape of one of the options from the Find Familiar spell. You also gain all the abilities of Find Familiar using your Automaton.
Level 4
You can infuse any non-magical item (including weapons and armor), with a magical effect. These effects can be from any 2nd level spell or less. If your DM allows, you may create a new effect or spell. Use the Spell Damage found in the Dungeon Master's Guide for determining damage.
Level 5
You can now infuse non-magical as well as magical items with a magical effect up to 4th level.

Level 3
Once per long rest, you gain the ability to gain advantage on an Attack roll. When you do this, you must roll on the Wild Magic Table, and take the effect at the end of your turn.
Level 4

Level 1
You have advantage on saving throws against being Charmed.
Level 2
Once per short rest, you gain the ability to entrance all enemies for 1 minute. Any enemy within line of sight of you has disavantage on Perception, Insight, Survival, and Intimidation.
Level 3
You can cast Charm Monster once per long rest. If you are able to cast spells, you learn this spell, which does not count towards your known spells.
Level 4
Your Charisma Score increases by 2, and gain proficiency with Charisma Saving throws. If you are already proficient, you are double proficient.
Level 5
At the end of a long rest, you may choose one of the following natural traits:
  • Glibness: Once per day you can cast Glibness on yourself without expending a spell slot.
  • Natural Charm: All creatures within 30 ft of you must make a Wisdom Saving Throw. The DC is 8 + Charisma Modifier + Proficiency Bonus. On a failed save, All hostile creatures treat you as neutral until you take a hostile action against them, and all Neutral creatures treat you as an ally until you take a hostile action against them. Once a creature is affected by this ability, they cannot be affected by this ability again for another 24 hours.
  • Sweet Talker: You gain the ability to freely cast Command.
Darkness Domain
Level 1
If you do not have darkvision, you gain darkvision up to 60 ft. If you have darkvision, your darkvision range is extended an additional 30 ft. You also gain proficiency with Stealth. If you are already proficient, you gain expertise.
Level 2
Once per long rest, you gain the ability to cast Darkness without the use of a spell slot. If you are able to cast spells, you learn this spell, which does not count towards your known spells.
Level 3
While in darkness or dim light, you gain the ability to cast Misty Step up to a number of time equal to your Wisdom Modifier, with a minimum of 1. You regain all uses of this ability at the end of a long rest.
Level 4
You can meld into the Shadows perfectly, practically become them. You can travel through darkness or dimlight at 4 times your normal movement speed. If you remain perfectly still in the shadows, you treat all stealth checks as if they were 20. When attacking from dim light or darkness, you deal an additional 1d6 damage.
Level 5
You can summon creatures from the darkness. Once per long rest, as an action, you can summon 1d4 Shadows in an unoccupied space within 10 ft. of you.
Death Domain
Level 1
At the end of a long rest, you gain Temporary Hit Points equal to your level.
Level 2
Creatures who you bring to fewer than 0 hit points, make their first Death Saving throw with disadvantage.
Level 3
Once per long rest, you can spend your action transforming into an Avatar of Death. You gain two of the following benefits for the next minute:
  • Deathless: You gain Temporary hit points equal to 1d8 + Constitution. These temporary hit points stack for the duration of the ability, but are lost once this effect ends
  • Death's Touch: You deal an additional 1d6 Necrotic damage on a successful Melee attacks.
  • Death's Reach: You deal an additional 1d6 Necrotic damage on a successful Ranged attacks.
  • Fear of Death: All creatures who can see you within 60 ft, must suceed a DC 16 Wisdom Saving throw, or become frightened for the duration of this ability. If a creature is successful, they are immune to this effect for 24 hours.
  • Deadened Mind: You are immune to being Frightened, Petrified, Charmed, or Stunned.
  • Deadened Body: You gain resistance to non-magical weapon damage made against you.
  • To Touch Death: All damage you deal is Necrotic damage.
Level 4
Your Avatar of Death state now lasts for 10 minutes, and can be used a number of times equal to your Constitution modifier.
Level 5
When gaining your Avatar of Death trait, you may now choose up to 4 of the following abilities. You may also not choose from the following list of abilities:
  • Superior Death's Touch: You deal an additional 2d6 Necrotic damage on a successful Melee attacks. This ability does not stack with Death's Touch.
  • Superior Death's Reach: You deal an additional 2d6 Necrotic damage on a successful Ranged attacks. This ability does not stack with Death's Reach.
  • Undeath: At the start of your Avatar of Death ability, you cast Create Undead on yourself at 6th level.

Law Domain
Level 1
As an action, you can touch a willing creature and grant them order in the chaos. For their next round, they treat all d20 rolls as if the results were an 11. You can do this a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier.
Level 2
Once per Long rest, as a free action, you can cause a creature to have disadvantage on a saving throw. The saving throw must be from an effect or a spell originating from you. This effect cannot be used on creatures who already have advantage or disadvantage.
Level 3
Once per Long rest, you can negate a special ability of a foe as a Standard action. In order to know a creature's abilities, you must succeed a DC10 + Monster CR Nature check. On a fail, the creature is unaffected. This effect lasts a number of rounds equal to your Wisdom modifier.
Level 4
Once per long rest, you can extend or reduce the duration of a spell effecting you by one category. Weeks <=> Days <=> Hours <=> Minutes <=> Rounds

You manifest your even hand of Law by executing Judgements. You can execute a Judgement on yourself, or on another creature as a bonus action.   • Resistance: Add your Wisdom modifer to the creatures AC.

  • Resiliency: Add your Wisdom modifer to all saving throws.
Luck Domain
Level 1
When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.
Level 2
As an action, you can touch a willing creature and grant them advantage on any attack roll, ability check and saving throw. You can use this ability a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1).
Level 3
Everything just seems to go your way. At the end of a long rest, you gain a number of d4's equal to your Wisdom Modifier (minimum 1) These are called your Lucky Dice. You may use these on any attack rolls, ability checks, or saving throws. You may add 1d4 to the roll before you learn the result.
Level 4
Your Lucky Dice are increased to a d6 instead of a d4. You also gain a number of d6's equal to double your Wisdom Modifier (minimum 1).
Level 5

You gain the ability to charge. In order to use this ability you must move at least half of your movement speed in a straight line, and be making a Melee attack against the creature. Only then can you Charge/Shield Bash an enemy. You deal an additional 2d6 damage as part of the attack.

Trickery Domain
Level 1
You gain proficiency with 2 of the following kills: Deception, Perception, Sleight of Hand, and Stealth. If you choose a proficiency you are already proficient in, you gain expertise. You also gain +1 to DEX and +1 to CHA.
Level 2
Once per long rest, you can create a perfect copy of yourself, a Doppelganger. The copy takes actions via a mental link, and can be commanded as a free action. The AC of the Copy is 11 + DEX, and has 1 HP. This ability does not effect creatures who have Blindsense, Tremorsense or True sight. You copy lasts for up to 10 minutes, or until destroyed.
Level 3
You gain the ability to disguise yourself at will. You can cast Disguise Self at will. You save DC is equal to 8 + Charisma modifier + Proficiency.
Level 4
You can now create a Doppelganger a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier. You can now swap places with your Doppelganger as a Reaction. Your Doppelganger lasts now up to an hour.
Level 5
Your Doppelganger's duration is until dispelled or killed. Your Doppelganger now also has an attack that it can make. It's attack is 1d8 + Your Dexterity modifier.
Void Domain
Level 1
You gain the ability to step in and out of the Ethereal Plane. You have a number of feet of movement equal to 10 x your Wisdom Modifier, with a minimum of 1. When moving, you must declare that you're entering the Ethereal plane. You do not provoke attacks of opportunity while in the Ethereal plane. You may pass through walls, but doing so is considered difficult terrain.
Level 2
At the end of a long rest, you can choose one of the following Void Abilities.
  • Ether Speak: Your verbal components when casting a spell goes into the Ethereal plane, and are inaudible to all those on the Material plane. Attempts to Counterspell are made at disadvantage.
  • Advanced Ether Step: Your distance is doubled for your Ether Step ability, making it a number of feet equal to 20 x your Wisdom Modifier, with a minimum of 1.
Level 3
As part of your Ether Step ability, you gain the ability to remain on the Ethereal plane, at the cost of 10 feet of your Ether step movement. You also gain the ability to drag another creature with you, for an additional 10 ft of movement. A creature reappears onto the Material plane when you appear back on the Material Plane. A creature can attempt to break free from your attempt with a Dexterity Saving throw. The DC is equal to 8 + Dexterity Modifer + Proficiency.
Level 4
Your Ether Step distance is doubled. This ability stacks with Advanced Ether Step. Your Wisdom also increases by 2
Level 5
At the end of a long rest, You can choose 2 from the following list, as well as the list from level 2, of Void Abilities.
  • Dimensional Door: You can teleport once per long rest, to anywhere on your current plane of existance.
  • Void Blur: As an action, you can partway enter the Void. On a successful attack against you, roll 1d10. On a 1 through 4, the attack misses.
War Domain
Level 1
You gain a +5 on initiaive rolls. You can't be surprised while you are conscious. You also don't gain advantage on Attack rolls against you as a result of not being seen. Your Strength increases by 2.
Level 2
When you make a successful attack against an enemy, they gain 1 point of Bleed damage. Enemies who are bleeding take the amount of bleed damage at the start of their turn. This ability stacks with itself. Bleed damage is cured with a DC15 Medicine Check or being healed by any method.
Level 3
When rolling damage, If a dice comes up with a 1 or a 2, reroll the damage, and roll again and you must take the new number. Your Charisma also increases by 2.
Level 4
You can attempt to intimidate all creatures in a 30 ft Cone as an Action. All creatures must make a Wisdom Saving throw, or become frightened for 2d6 rounds. The DC for the save is equal to 6 + Strength Modifider + Charisma Modifier + Proficiency bonus.
Level 5
Your vicious attacks now deal 1d4 points of bleed damage instead of 1. Your Strength also increases by 2.
Water Domain
Level 1
You gain a swim speed equal to your land movement speed. You also gain the ability to breathe underwater and on land.
Level 2
You gain the use of the Ice Knife spell. If you are a spellcaster, this does not count towards your spells known. Your Wisdom Increases by 2.
Level 3
You gain the ability to guild yourself in Ice. The ice armor surrounding you has a Hit Point total of 10 x Wisdom Modifier. Once the armor is broken, all creatures, including yourself, must make a Dexterity saving throw. The DC is 8 + Wisdom modifier. On a failed save, they take 1d10 Cold damage, and half as much on a successful save.
Level 4
Your Ice Armor's AC is now equal to 2 + your current AC. It's hit point total also increases to 20 x Wisdom Modifier. Your Wisdom also Increases by 2.
Level 5
You can create water in abnormal volumes. As a reaction, you can create a surge of water around you. Every creature within 20 ft of you, must make a Strength Saving throw. The DC is equal to 8 + Wisdom Modifier + Proficiency. If a creature fails, they are pushed back to the edge of the area of effect. If they fail by more than 5, they are also knocked prone. You can use this ability a number of times equal to your Wisdom Modifier.
